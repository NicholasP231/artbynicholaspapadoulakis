My HUW 162 (Animation) Final Exam Preperation


My three versions of my planning HUW 162 (animation) final exam (fan-made animation) Neo F-Zero Grand-Prix (Futuristic Grand-Prix Theme)

(1) In its 3D version, I used Shade Editor and UV Editor to put realistic shadings on images, like the image of Captain Falcon and the background of the red metal floor and wall; emissive texts, an emissive arrow and emissive lightings to bring brightness, and Editing Mode to make those futuristic racing machines and background detailed on 3D viewpoint mode.

(2) In its toon version, I used Shader to RGB, Separate RGB, and ColorRamp on shader editor on all the materials except the emissive lights, arrow, and letters.

(3) In its white emissive version, I took out all the colors of each material, I turned them into white emissive, and used Freestyle dark blue lines.
