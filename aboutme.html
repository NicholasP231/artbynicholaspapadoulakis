About Me






Hello everyone. My name is Nicholas Papadoulakis from Greece, and I live in an island which is called Crete. From the age of three until now I like drawing. In the future I want to become an animator. My dream is to graduate from a good college here in the United States and learn more skills about animation. I hopefully can't wait to work as an animator in one of America's animation studios. I study New Media Technology at LaGuardia Community College. Also, I took Fine Art Courses.
